Who am I?

My name is Mathias and I’m a multimedia design student from Odense, Denmark.

As a multimedia enthusiast and digital nerd, I have an affinity for technology. I use this affinity to take on new challenges and learn from these, giving me the toolkit to take on bigger and more difficult projects.

As a competitive-minded person I always strive to be the best and, create the best product possible.

My vision

I aspire to create a fresh and different feeling.

Combining user familiarity with a touch of creativity, my goal is to create something which hasn’t been seen before. Apart from that I always make sure to deliver high quality products, tailored for your need.

My process

To achieve my goals, I draw from my creativity.

It’s vital for me to find an enviorment where i can let my mind work and do its own thing, usually accompanied by a collection of my favorite tunes.

When I’m in the ‘zone’ I won’t stop until I’m satisfied, which unfortunately is a difficult task for me.
